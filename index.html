ICT 11-2

Dos is a dynamic and engaging website created specifically for students in the ICT 11-2 section. This platform offers a space for students to share their thoughts, feelings, and rants anonymously or publicly, and connect with their peers in a safe and moderated environment. With a user-friendly interface, Dos makes it easy to share and engage with content, whether it's a personal reflection, a humorous anecdote, or a message of encouragement for the whole class. One of the standout features of Dos is the ability to post announcements for the entire class, making it a valuable tool to remind everyone. Dos prioritizes the privacy and security of its users, providing a secure and monitored environment for students to connect and interact.
